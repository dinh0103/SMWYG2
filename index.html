How to Play PEAK

A beginner’s guide to surviving a climbing friendslop game because who doesn't love friendslop!

What Is PEAK?

PEAK is a 3D game on Steam that costs $7.99. The island map generation changes daily so there's always a new challenge each day.

You are lost on a mysterious island with friends and you have to scale all the way to the end to escape. However as you travel further, there will be obstacles trying to kill you before you reach the end.

Some players refer to games like Peak as “friendslop,” a term used for multiplayer-focused games designed around playing with friends to create funny moments and shareable clips. These games often gain popularity for a few months before fading out, but they remain a fun and memorable experience.

Game Objectives

  • Follow the rules the Scoutmaster has laid out for you when you first crash land.
  • Your objective is to stay alive and work together to get through many challenges of each biome. You have to go quick before the fog catches up to you.
  • There are suitcases scattered around the map filled with random items to help your journey.

Basic Controls

  • You move around with the WASD keys and look around with your mouse.
  • Any vertical wall you can grab and climb by holding the left click button. Press space bar to lunge.
  • Your character can jump with spacebar.

Core Mechanics

  • You have limited stamina that can regenerate and it decreases as you get hungry or get poisoned.
  • Falling from any tall height will take out a chunk of stamina you can't recover unless you find bandages.
  • Your inventory has 3 slots and one for your backpack which can hold 4 more items. They all have weight that can take up your stamina bar.

Multiplayer & Teamwork

Your friends can lend you a hand if they are already at the top of the ledge but you can also ration out items between each other.

Having friends change the experience some may work with or against you depending on how serious they want to play. Overall it makes a great team-building game to have a laugh with friends!

How to Win

  1. Start climbing the rocks as you walk forward because there's only one general direction to proceed.
  2. Manage your stamina and positioning as you climb each small area bit by bit.
  3. You reach the peak once you successfully climb up the volcano crater.

Beginner Tips

  • Foods cooked by the campfire give additional stamina.
  • You can fill up an entire second stamina bar if you keep eating extra, the second bar won't regenerate once used.
  • Always keep an eye out for the suitcases!
  • DO NOT STARVE OR ELSE YOUR CAMPMATES WILL TRANSFORM....(click me)

Why PEAK Is Fun

You get to play with friends for badges and there's fun silly character customization options before the climb! The more badges you earn, the more cosmetics unlocked as well!
